The Pigpiss Chronicles

  1. Go to Half life 2 in steam
  2. Click the cog and then go to Manage > browse local files
  3. Open the hl2 folder
  4. Open the maps folder
  5. Copy the maps from the zip into the maps folder
  6. Open hl2
  7. Open the dev console
  8. Type map pigpiss01
  9. Enjoy the game.
