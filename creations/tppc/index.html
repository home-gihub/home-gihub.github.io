The Pigpiss Chronicles

What the hell is The Pigpiss Chronicles??? TPPC is a Half-life 2 map series I made based on an inside joke me and my friend made based on a video by Matt Rose. We just found that the word sounded funny and everything sprouted from there. Eventually I got the idea to make a Half-life 2 map pack based on it. And so, TPPC was born. If your not in the loop, you might not understand any of the jokes/references in the game. But I've put it here on my website just incase anyone would like to play.
